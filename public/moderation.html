Content Policy

Guidelines for community submissions

What We Encourage
  • Honest, constructive feedback about your experiences
  • Helpful advice for fellow graduates
  • Accurate information about application processes
  • Professional and respectful language
What Is Not Allowed
  • False or misleading information
  • Personal attacks or harassment
  • Defamatory content about individuals
  • Confidential or proprietary information
  • Spam or promotional content
Moderation

We review submissions to ensure they meet our guidelines. Content that violates our policies may be removed.

Reporting

If you see content that violates our policies, please report it and we will review it promptly.
