Content & Moderation Policy

Last updated: {{ config.LAST_UPDATED }}

We're committed to maintaining a safe, respectful community for sharing graduate experiences.

✓ What's Welcome

  • Genuine graduate program experiences and insights
  • Helpful career advice and application tips
  • Respectful discussions about companies and universities
  • Constructive feedback and honest reviews
  • Questions seeking advice or clarification

✗ What's Not Allowed

  • Personal Information (Doxxing): Don't share anyone's private contact details, addresses, or personal information without consent
  • Personal Attacks: No harassment, bullying, or targeted abuse of individuals
  • Hate Speech: Content targeting people based on race, religion, gender, sexuality, or other protected characteristics
  • Illegal Content: Anything that violates Australian law
  • Confidential Information: Don't share confidential company information, internal documents, or proprietary data
  • Defamatory Claims: False statements that could damage someone's reputation
  • Spam: Repetitive, irrelevant, or promotional content
  • Copyright Infringement: Don't post content you don't own or have permission to share

Pre-Moderation for High-Risk Content

To protect individuals and organisations, we may pre-moderate posts that:

  • Name specific individuals with allegations of misconduct
  • Make serious claims about company practices
  • Contain potential defamatory statements

These posts will be reviewed before publication to ensure they comply with our policies and Australian law.

Notice-and-Takedown Process

How It Works:

  1. Report Content: Use our report form with specific details
  2. Acknowledgment: We'll acknowledge your report within 48 hours
  3. Assessment: We may temporarily restrict access to content while investigating
  4. Investigation: We'll contact the poster for their perspective when appropriate
  5. Decision: We'll decide whether to remove, modify, or restore content with clear reasons
  6. Appeal: Both parties can appeal our decision through {{ config.CONTACT_EMAIL }}

Service Levels:

  • Urgent safety matters: Immediate action
  • Standard reports: Response within 48 hours
  • Complex legal matters: Up to 7 business days

Enforcement Actions

Depending on the severity and frequency of violations, we may:

  • Warning: First-time minor violations
  • Content Removal: Remove specific posts or comments
  • Temporary Restriction: Limit posting abilities for a period
  • Account Suspension: Temporary suspension for serious or repeated violations
  • Permanent Ban: For severe violations or users who repeatedly breach policies

Appeals Process

If you disagree with a moderation decision:

  1. Email {{ config.CONTACT_EMAIL }} within 30 days
  2. Include the specific content and reasons for your appeal
  3. We'll review the decision with fresh eyes
  4. You'll receive a response within 7 business days
